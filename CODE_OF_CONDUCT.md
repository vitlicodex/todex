# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be precise when discussing risks.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Do not post private tokens, logs, paths, screenshots, or prompt contents.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing exploit details in public issues when responsible disclosure is appropriate.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.
